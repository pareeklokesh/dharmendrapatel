MY STORY

On 24th January, in the year 1971, Kantibhai Patel and Arunaben Patel were blessed with a baby boy whom they named Dharmendra at Khed Brahma Village in the District of Sabarkantha. Kantibhai Patel being in the Top Position of Crime branch in Gujarat Police, so he had to travel to different parts of Gujarat hence Mr. Dharmendra studied in various parts of Gujarat including Baroda, Palanpur and Ahmadabad. For post schooling Mr. Dharmendra went to Nav Gujarat College, Ahmadabad. He was active in University activities and was a Senate Member of Gujarat University.

Equine Life
